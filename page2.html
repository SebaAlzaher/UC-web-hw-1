Crash Bandicoot is a popular American video game series published by Activision, developed by Naughty Dog, and distributed by Sony Computer Entertainment. Initially created by Andy Gavin and Jason Robin, the series was developed in its first four years by video game company Naughty Dog. After that the series was given to different developers
